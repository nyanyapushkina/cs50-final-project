How to use LEARNBYHEART

This is a simple web application for kids to help them memorize poetry for kindergarten, or school, or just for fun!


1. Type in the poem you want to learn into the white block on the main page. Make sure to keep indentation: each line of the poem should be on the separeate line! The application won't work, if you type in the poem as one long paragraph

2. Click on the orange 'Learn' button

3. There will be 4 cards on the screen. Hover your mouse over a card to flip it over and read a part of the poem. Move the mouse away to check yourself and try to recite the part of the poem

4. Keep flipping the cards until you learn the poem!
